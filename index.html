What is Mean opinion score ?

(MOS) is a measure used in the domain of Quality of Experience and telecommunications engineering, representing overall quality of a stimulus or system. The Mean Opinion Score Values are taken in whole numbers, the numbers are quite easy to grade.
5 - Excellent 4 - Good 3 - Fair. 2 - Poor 1 - Bad

How Are MOS Tests Conducted?

A certain number of people are sat and are made to hear some audio. Each one of them gives a rating from within 1 to 5. Then an arithmetic mean (average) is calculated, giving the Mean Opinion Score. When conducting MOS test, there are certain phrases that are recommended to be used by the ITU-T. They are:
  • You will have to be very quiet.
  • There was nothing to be seen.
  • They worshiped wooden idols.
  • I want a minute with the inspector.
  • Did he need any money?
