What is Mean opinion score ?

(MOS) is a measure used in the domain of Quality of Experience and telecommunications engineering, representing overall quality of a stimulus or system. The Mean Opinion Score Values are taken in whole numbers, the numbers are quite easy to grade.
5 - Excellent 4 - Good 3 - Fair. 2 - Poor 1 - Bad
Similarity Score Between two waveforms
5 - Very Similar 4 - Fairly Similar 3 - Somewhat similar 2 - Fairly Different 1 - Completely Different

How Are MOS Tests Conducted?

A certain number of people are sat and are made to hear some audio. Each one of them gives a rating from within 1 to 5. Then an arithmetic mean (average) is calculated, giving the Mean Opinion Score.

High Quality Headphones are used when listening to the waveforms to clearly distinquish between synthesized and target wave.
